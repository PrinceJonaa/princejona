## 2025-05-02 - Immersive Navigation Traps
**Learning:** In immersive, story-driven interfaces (like "Prince Jona"), removing global navigation to increase immersion creates "dead ends" for users who want to explore non-linearly.
**Action:** When designing immersive pages, always include a subtle, non-intrusive "exit" or "back" anchor to maintain flow without breaking the spell.
